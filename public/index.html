Welcome!

Our mission at Bell Vision Optometry is preserving the gift of sight through the highest quality of care. We treat every patient like our own family member and believe in creating a happier and healthier community together.

Dr. Janet Ko

Dr. Ko was born and raised in Simi Valley, CA and moved to the East Bay to study Molecular Cell Biology at UC Berkeley. She loved the Bay Area so much she stayed to attend the UC Berkeley School of Optometry, where she completed her optometry training and became certified to treat glaucoma. Dr. Ko loves working with kids and provides vision screenings at various schools in Alameda/Contra Costa Counties. She loves to spend her free time volunteering at her church, trying new restaurants, and camping at June Lake with her family. Dr. Ko welcomes you to Bell Vision Optometry and looks forward to meeting you.

Dr. Vy Mai

Dr. Vy Mai received her undergraduate degree from Oregon State University and her Doctor of Optometry from Pennsylvania College of Optometry. During her optometric training, she gained extensive experience with the geriatric population and diagnosing age-related macular degeneration, cataract, diabetic retinopathy, retinal detachment and epiretinal membrane during her Veteran Hospital rotations. She is glaucoma certified and does ortho-keratology. She loves to bake and cook but her end products are sometimes not so edible.

Dr. Michelle Wong

Dr. Michelle Wong was born and raised in the beautiful Bay Area. She attended UC Davis for her undergraduate degree and later attended Nova Southeastern University in Ft. Lauderdale, Florida where she graduated with a B.S. in Vision and and her Doctor of Optometry degree. Her training includes pediatrics, contact lens, ocular disease, as well as rotations at the world renown Bascom Palmer Eye Institute. She is board certified and glaucoma certified. In her free time, she enjoys hiking and spending time with her friends and family.
